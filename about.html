Order Set and Product Management Guide

Learn how to manage your order sets, add products, and track profit/loss

Overview of Features

This website allows you to manage up to 11 different order sets, add products to each set, calculate profit/loss, and store all the information locally so that your data persists between visits.

1. Getting Started

Selecting an Order Set: Use the dropdown to select a set (1–11). Each set represents a different group of products or an order batch.

Entering Set Cost: After selecting the order set, enter the cost of the entire set in the input field provided.

Saving Set: Click the "Save Set" button to store the information in local storage. You can view and manage this set later.

2. Managing Products in Each Set

Adding Products: For each order set, you can add individual products by entering the name, cost, and sold price of the product. Click "Save Product" to add the product to the order set.

Product Collapse: Each product added will be displayed in a collapsible section for easier management and editing.

3. Profit and Loss Calculation

Automatic Calculation: Profit and loss are calculated based on the total cost and the total sold price of the products in each set. The formula used is:

Profit/Loss = Total Sold Price - Total Product Costs

4. Saving and Persistence

All your sets and products are stored in your browser's local storage. This means that even if you refresh the page or close your browser, your data will still be there when you return.

5. FAQs & Troubleshooting

Q: Why is my data not saved after refreshing the page?

Ensure that your browser allows local storage and hasn't cleared its data recently. If local storage is disabled, data will not persist.

Q: Can I edit products after adding them?

Currently, products cannot be edited once saved. You would need to remove them and add a new one.

Q: What if the profit/loss isn't displaying?

Make sure you’ve added at least one product with valid cost and sold price values.
